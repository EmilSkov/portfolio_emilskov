Escape the grid

24hours game project

We were a group of 3 people who got an idea of making a mobile game, we had many different ideas for a game, but we decided that it would be a game for the endlessrunner genre.

We were brainstorming about how the virsuelle should look like and what game mechanics should be in the game.

The game ended up being in retro style and was made for android mobiles and could be downloaded from the google play store.
